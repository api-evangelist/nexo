Crypto trades like a risk asset, and the Fed sets the price of risk for the whole economy, so when that price changes money moves and crypto is one of the places it moves to or from. Federal Reserve rate decisions influence crypto through the cost of borrowing, the opportunity cost of holding volatile assets versus bonds, and the strength of the dollar, with surprises rather than expected decisions driving the largest moves.
