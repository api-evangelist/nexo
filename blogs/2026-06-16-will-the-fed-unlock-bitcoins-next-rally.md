For months, uncertainty around the U.S.-Iran conflict has weighed on risk assets and capped every Bitcoin recovery attempt. Over the weekend, reports of a ceasefire agreement offered some relief as oil prices fell sharply, Asian equities moved higher, and Bitcoin climbed back above $65,000 for the first time in nearly two weeks. The dispatch also covers the first decision under the new Fed chair and SpaceX's Bitcoin going public.
